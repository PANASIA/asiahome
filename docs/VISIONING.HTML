Visioning

Visioning initiates design and builds the company’s vision into a business model. No migration is carried out, because the developed model is intended to serve as a generic model that is implemented in the specific sites.

  • Mapping

In this mapping stage the program/project mission is drawn up. In addition, the scope of the Business Control Model (draft) and the models are completed and accepted.

  • Piloting

In Piloting an assessment and a description of the organizational impact caused by the designed Business Control Model are made. This stage results in the final business model.
