Proof of Concept

Proof of Concept refers to the pilot implementation to test the results of the previous phase in a real life situation.

  • Mapping

In this stage the scope of the Business Control Model and the models are completed and accepted.

  • Piloting

In Piloting an assessment and a description of the organizational impact caused by the designed Business Control Model are made. This stage results in the final business model.

  • Migration

Migration is the stage in which the systems and data are transferred and the system, personnel and organization in the pilot site become operational.
