Selection

Selection includes a set of milestones required to configure the business solution and the functional solution for the customer.

  • Orientation

Orientation is a predefined set of milestones that supports a customer in the introduction of UDS, the prospect and the mutual qualification of the potential business solution, cultural compatibility, and technical viability of a UDS Implementation in the enterprise.

  • Solution

Within the solution stage, a UDS solution in terms of hardware, software and services is defined. Furthermore the basis for a sound commercial relationship is established.

  • Resolution

The Resolution stage encompasses the completion of the sales cycle, resulting in a newly signed customer ready to embark upon implementation.
